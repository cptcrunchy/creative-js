Curriculum Development

CCILS team aims to help you design a custom curriculum tailored to meet the specific needs of your organization. Whether it is starting with the needs analysis phase to identify your requirements, or assessing gaps in your current curriculum, we apply the best instructional design strategies in the design to create effective training with high learner retention levels.

Learn More

Curriculum Board Review:

All members of our curriculum development team have experience in instructional design and foreign language education. Under the supervision of the advisory board our work is customized to our client’s needs, CCILS constructs curriculum to incorporate a variety of training delivery methods to create a successful program.

Instructional Innovative Design Education Program:

Our evaluation services include the study of the techniques for the effectiveness of contextual structure analysis and instructional/educational programs including development of written and performance-based tests as well as questionnaires. It ensures validation and reliability of tools covering a wide range of subjects such as sociology, psychology, linguistics, and education.
