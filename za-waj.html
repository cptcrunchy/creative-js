Cross Cultural Instructional Languages Services

provides essential cross-cultural and competency-based language training, interpretation, translation services, diagnostic assessments and curriculum development, and educational support for any language and culture. Our united expertise covers a range of services in the field of our linguist services include: Language Localization, Interpretation, Translation, Trans-creation, Assessment, Advisory, Mechanical Expertise, and Language Instruction. We cover all levels of language using a proficiency approach. Our services enable government agencies, communities, and local businesses the opportunity to operate successfully in a global environment.
